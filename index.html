Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our solar solutions and services

What are the benefits of installing solar panels?

Installing solar panels helps reduce electricity bills, decreases your carbon footprint, and provides a reliable energy source. Additionally, solar energy can increase property value and offer long-term savings.

How do solar inverters work?

Solar inverters convert the direct current (DC) electricity generated by solar panels into alternating current (AC) electricity, which can be used by your home's appliances or fed back into the grid.

What is the maintenance required for solar panels?

Solar panels require minimal maintenance. Regular cleaning to remove dust and debris, along with periodic inspections by a professional, ensures optimal performance. Solifex offers comprehensive maintenance and after-sales services to keep your system running efficiently.

Can solar power be used during power outages?

Yes, with our hybrid and off-grid solar solutions, you can store solar energy in batteries to use during power outages. This ensures a continuous power supply for critical loads, such as lights, refrigerators, and other essential appliances.

What financing options are available for solar installations?

Solifex offers various financing options to make solar energy accessible to everyone. We provide flexible payment plans, leasing options, and can assist with obtaining government incentives and rebates.
