At Solifex, we believe that our commitment to customers extends beyond the completion of a solar panel installation. Our dedicated technical team ensures that your solar system operates at peak performance through regular monitoring of power generation. Should any reduction in solar power be detected, we will promptly address the issue to minimize downtime. We utilize specialized equipment for safe and effective solar panel cleaning to enhance their lifespan and efficiency. Additionally, we offer maintenance services for panels installed by other providers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about solar panel maintenance and after-sales services.

Do solar panels need maintenance?

Solar panels are generally low maintenance, but regular upkeep ensures optimal performance. Over time, dust and debris can affect efficiency. Our team at Solifex is ready to help with maintenance and cleaning to keep your system running smoothly, whether we installed the panels or not.

How often should solar panels be serviced?

We recommend servicing solar panels every 6 months to ensure they are performing at their best.

What are the maintenance costs for solar panels?

Maintenance costs can vary depending on the site. For a detailed quote, please contact Solifex.
