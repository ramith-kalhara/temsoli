Solar Panel Setup

Solifex places a high priority on our solar panel setup process, focusing on customer satisfaction and convenience. We leverage the expertise of our technical team – consisting of over 30 highly experienced engineers – to deliver comprehensive turnkey solar solutions for all our clients, encompassing everything from the initial site survey to the design, solar panel installation, commissioning, and maintenance of the solar systems. A dedicated engineer is appointed to each project from the outset to ensure that quality standards and deadlines are met throughout the entire solar panel setup process.
We go to great lengths to customize and tailor all our solar panel setups and solar system projects to meet our clients' requirements, accommodating various mounting methods such as tile, asbestos or amano roofs, concrete slabs, as well as ground-mounted solar systems.

Here’s how we carry out our solar panel installations in Sri Lanka, step by step:

  • Initial site visit and inspection
  • Designing the most optimum solution giving due consideration to the client’s needs
  • Obtaining necessary clearances from CEB/LECO
  • Installation of equipment
  • Testing and commissioning
  • Grid connection

Frequently Asked Questions

Your guide to understanding solar panel installation

What factors should I consider before installing solar panels at my home?

  • Available space on the roof and its structural strength
  • The amount of sunlight the roof receives
  • Choosing a reliable company for solar power system installation

How much space do I need on my roof for solar panels?

The space required depends on your energy needs and the efficiency of the panels. On average, a residential solar system needs about 100-300 square feet.

How do I determine if my roof gets enough sunlight for solar panels?

A solar professional can assess your roof’s exposure to sunlight. Generally, south-facing roofs with minimal shading are ideal for solar panels.

What is the average cost of installing solar panels?

The cost varies depending on the size of the system and your location. On average, residential solar panel systems can cost between $10,000 and $25,000 before any tax credits or incentives.

How long does it take to install solar panels?

The installation process typically takes 1-3 days, but the entire process, including permits and inspections, can take a few weeks to a few months.

What maintenance is required for solar panels?

Solar panels require minimal maintenance. It's recommended to clean them a few times a year and have a professional inspection every 1-2 years to ensure optimal performance.
