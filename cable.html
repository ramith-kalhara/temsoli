Cables

Features of cables

  • Efficiency: Reduces energy loss.
  • Safety: Minimizes electrical faults and hazards.
  • Longevity: Withstands environmental stress.
  • Compliance: Meets international standards.

High Quality Cables

We are committed to delivering top-notch solar power solutions. A crucial aspect of our installations is the use of high-quality cables, ensuring safety, efficiency, and longevity of our systems. We source our DC, AC, and earth cables from industry-leading manufacturers, including Lapp, KBE, ACL, Kelani, and Sierra.

Our Trusted Cable Suppliers

  • Lapp

    • Lapp is renowned for its innovative cable solutions and extensive product range. They provide cables that are designed to withstand extreme environmental conditions, ensuring reliable performance for our solar installations.

  • KBE

    • KBE specializes in cables for the photovoltaic industry, offering products that meet international standards and provide optimal performance in solar power systems.

  • ACL

    • ACL cables are known for their superior quality and durability, making them ideal for both residential and commercial solar power systems.

  • Kelani

    • Kelani Cables are a preferred choice for their reliability and cost-effectiveness, providing excellent performance in various solar applications.

  • Sierra

    • Sierra offers a comprehensive range of cables that are specifically designed for the demands of solar power systems, ensuring safety and efficiency.
